G9 Helper

Crisp HiDPI scaling for Samsung Odyssey G9 and other large monitors. Free and open source.

Download for macOS
v1.0.0 macOS 12+ | Apple Silicon & Intel | 1.4 MB

Installation

1Open the DMG
2Drag to Applications
3Launch from menu bar
First launch: Right-click the app, select "Open", then click "Open" in the dialog. This is required for apps outside the App Store.

Supported Monitors

Monitor Native HiDPI
Samsung G9 57" 7680x2160 5120x1440 *
Samsung G9 49" 5120x1440 3840x1080 *
34" Ultrawide 3440x1440 2560x1080
4K Displays 3840x2160 2560x1440 *

Limitations

  • 60Hz refresh rate when HiDPI mirroring active
  • HDR may not work in mirrored mode
  • Base M-chips: max 6144px horizontal
  • Pro/Max/Ultra: full 7680px support
